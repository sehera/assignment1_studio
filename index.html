Concept: Borders come with rigidity, tension, differences and similarities. I wanted to express such subjects through a set of three colours, only using
three fonts correlating to three text sources and using mainly typography with minimal graphics. Mainly because I want complete focus to be on the words
and their meaning. As you scroll through the page there are variations and differences of presentation that show contrast of this subject, the contrasting opinions
and spectrums of how much one if affected by borders. However at the end of the day there is still a sense of unity that canbe found within the presentation of text.
Red and black are high contrast and bold colors with the white and subtle blue finding a middleground in that spectrum. I also wanted to create a subtle
escalating experience as the type gets slowly more emphasized towards the other half of the page, to represent the urgency of this subject.
